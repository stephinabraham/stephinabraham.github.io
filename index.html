Welcome to Holy Cross




VISION AND MISSION


To train young people in order to become effectively an important part of the efficient health care team by imparting the professional knowledge in the area of Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology (D.M.L.T) and Diploma in Medical Imaging Technology (D.M.I.T.) To give an overall development for the young person under training based on moral and social principles and values. To give young person a professional training so that he/she becomes competent in the medical profession that he/she engaged in.

Courses Offered


DMLT – Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology. Intake capacity of students 20(16+4)

DMXT – Diploma in X-ray Technology. Intake capacity of students 20 (16+4)

Qualification : Two years for PUC passed with science subject which includes Biology. Three year for SSLC and for all PUC passed or failed with non science subject. In the three year course, in first year basic Science and English will be taught. There will be annual exam after each year conducted by Para Medical Board.

PRINCIPAL’S MESSAGE

It gives immense pleasure to write about Holy Cross Institute of
Para Medical Sciences. It is my privilege that I am associated with this Institution as Principal from its inception in the year 2000. This institution, I can say without hesitation is unique in imparting quality education. It is the only Institution in the district to have well qualified and experienced teaching staff. It is governed by the Society of Sisters of the Holy Cross, who are dedicated, disciplined, and honest and selfless in serving the Society. With this background, the students trained here will, not only get the academic qualification but also learn good discipline and develop good characters. These are the fundamental need of all individuals in their formative age of adolescence. Strict discipline also brings intolerance to misbehaviour. Therefore all the students should be mentally prepared for a rigorous course which is beneficial to them in their life. I welcome all those with a right set of mind to come and join the course to get the best that can be offered in Para Medical Course.

Dr. Khalid A. Khan
