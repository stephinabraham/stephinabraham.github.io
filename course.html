Mandatory Internship

As per rule of Paramedical Board a 3 months internship is compulsory after passing the theory and practical examination. After the completion of internship only a student is eligible to get the course completion Certificate. The management will decide the place of their internship. No stipend will be given during the period of internship. The decision of the Principal/ Management in all matters shall be final and binding on all the students. The Management reserves the right to change the rules and regulations from time to time as required.

Fee Payment

Total annual fees should be paid on the day of admission. These fees are non-refundable. Second and third year annual fees should be paid by 31st January of that academic year. Fees structure is subject to change.

Role Of Parents

We welcome the Parents to come and meet the staff and their wards whenever it is convenient for them. Parents can contact the Principal/management to know their progress of their wards. Parents meeting will be arranged twice a year and it is mandatory for the parents to participate in their meeting.
