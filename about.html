About Us

Brief background about Holy Cross Institute of Paramedical Sciences

Jyothinagar,Chikkamagaluru


The institute was established in the year 2000 under Christian Medical Association of India (CMAI). Because of technical reasons, this course was stopped in the year 2008, subsequently we obtained the permission, after fulfilling all the formalities to start the Karnataka State Government sponsored course in Diploma In Medical Laboratory technology (DMLT) in the year 2010, and later in the year 2017, Diploma In Medical X-ray Technology (DMXT) was started. The Institute comes under Holy Cross Hospital, Chikkamagaluru, which is administered by the Society of Sisters of the Holy Cross. It is situated about 2.5 Kms from the centre of City, in the Chikkamagaluru-kadur Road. It is a 150 bedded Hospital with all the main branches of Medicine, with a well-equipped Laboratory having fully automated chemistry analyser, blood cell counter, blood gas analyser and licensed blood bank with component separation facility. Well - experienced, qualified technologists to manage the lab and computerised X-ray department round the clock 24x7.
